Q: I've never used Git. What should I use?
A: Use push-to-github.sh/.bat (automated) OR PUSH_NOW.md (simple 3 steps)

Q: I don't like running scripts. What should I use?
A: Use PUSH_NOW.md (only 3 steps, all manual)

Q: I want to understand what's happening. What should I use?
A: Use START_HERE_GITHUB.md (explains everything)

Q: I'm experienced with Git. What should I use?
A: Use GITHUB_QUICK_REFERENCE.txt (just the commands)

Q: Something went wrong. What should I use?
A: Check VERIFICATION_CHECKLIST.md and PUSH_TO_GITHUB_NOW.md (troubleshooting)

Q: I already pushed. How do I verify it worked?
A: Use VERIFICATION_CHECKLIST.md (after-push section)
